Executive Summary

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is transforming global insurance. With a projected CAGR of 21.5%, AI will enable new levels of underwriting, risk profiling, customer personalization, and operational efficiency. This report offers a strategic perspective on the AI Insurance landscape, leveraging insights from global adoption data, funding trends, regulatory shifts, and pioneering case studies—including a special spotlight on China's digital ecosystem.

6. Strategic Imperatives for Insurers

3. Fintech Partnerships

Leverage embedded offerings via ecommerce, banks, and digital wallets.

4. Workforce Augmentation

Train underwriters and agents on GenAI co-pilots; redefine role of human intelligence.
